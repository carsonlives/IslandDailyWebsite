About Island Daily

Island Daily is a short, daily podcast for people who live and work on Vancouver Island.

What We Cover

Each episode delivers a concise roundup of local news, city council decisions, weather updates, traffic alerts, and community events — designed to be easy to listen to and easy to share.

Why It Exists

Local journalism matters. Island Daily is built to keep Vancouver Islanders connected to their communities — one morning at a time.
